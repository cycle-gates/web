About us

Welcome to Cycle Gates, your one-stop destination for all things cycling! We are an Egyptian company that is dedicated to providing a wide range of cycling services to our clients.

Our goal

Our goal is to put in people's minds the idea that when they think of cycling, they think of Cycle Gates. We strive to be the go-to destination for all things cycling in Egypt and beyond. At Cycle Gates, we believe that cycling is not just a form of exercise but a way of life. That's why we offer a variety of cycling services that cater to different needs and interests. Whether you're a beginner or a seasoned cyclist, we have something for everyone. At Cycle Gates, we are passionate about cycling and are committed to providing the best cycling services to our customers. Join us today and discover the joy of cycling!
